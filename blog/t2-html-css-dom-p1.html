  2. CSS:

What is meant by boxifying design?

Veiwing your webpage as various boxes to arrange content even if the box is an unusual shape like a triangle.

What is the box model?

It is a design idea that shows what html element is and isn't within your box.
content: what is inside the box like text
padding: the space between content and the border box
border: the border of the box
margin: the space between this box and the other boxes
