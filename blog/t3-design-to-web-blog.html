Web Design

What is a responsive site , and why responsiveness is important?

A responsive site is a website that automatically changes its content to fit the screen it is being viewed on. This is important because orginally people only looked on monitors and nowadays people tend to look on websites on the go on their phones.

What is a mobile first design, and why it's important?

A mobile first design is a website that is desgned for mobiles first, rather than desktops. This is so that important content can work well on small screens first and be built up to work well on bigger screens as well. This is important because people tend to look on website on their phones more and more making it important to get the display correct.

What are frameworks and, what are their pros and cons?

A framework is like a template which can be used to standardise code and solve common issues.

Pros

  1. Browser compatibility

  2. Clean and Tidy

  3. Saves you Time

  4. Solutions to Issues

Cons

  1. Can't learn to do it yourself

  2. Less control

What is a wireframe and why do we use it?

A wireframe is a sketch of the layout of the website. We do this so we have an understanding on what we will be doing.

What are the aspects of your wireframes you found difficult to implement, and why?

I found resizing the most difficult, I kept getting resizing parts but not the whole page evenly. Also getting it to look neat and tidy I am still not overly happy with it but I am at the time limit.
