Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learned that I could talk a lot more freely from the the mindful conversation. Also I retain a lot more information when I was listen. The other person found it to be the same, although it was a little bit awkward at first to get in to the flow of things.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Take one breathe a day, or do less than you can. I think it depends on what the day and your motivation. Some days meditations seems more of chore and less of a relaxation so I tend to not do it. So one of these techniques will help.

Any other musings?

I found that taking one mindful breathe a day or more is really helpful. Espcially if you are about to a hard climb or getting really frustrated trying a solve a problem it really clears the mind.
